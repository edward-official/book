Little Endian (32-bit integer): 0x12345678

1. General notation
   - 0x12 (most significant byte, MSB)
   - 0x78 (least significant byte, LSB)

2. Big Endian
   - Low address → high address
   - Storage order: 12 34 56 78

3. Little Endian
   - Low address → high address
   - Storage order: 78 56 34 12

4. Characteristics
   - Big Endian: matches the order humans read (from the largest byte first)
   - Little Endian: stored in reverse (starting from the smallest byte first)
